    Who is the longest-serving manager in Premier League history?

    Sir Alex Ferguson is the longest-serving manager in Premier League history. The Scottish international was at the helm of Manchester United from 1986 to 2013, when he finally retired. He won thirteen Premier League titles and five FA Cups with the side. He also won 2 Champions League trophies.
