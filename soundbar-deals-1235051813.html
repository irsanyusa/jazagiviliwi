    One of the most important aspects of any home theater is a premium sound system, one that allows for an immersive auditory experience for any show or movie you decide to binge from the living room couch.

    But not everyone has the space — or money — for a full home audio system. That’s where a good soundbar comes in. Slim and sleek, these portable speakers deliver impressive sound for their size, often going above and beyond what you get from your TV’s speakers alone.

    And if you were looking to upgrade your at-home viewing experience, now is the time to make the splurge with a number of top soundbar deals online, including best-selling units from LG, Bose and Sony for up to 81% off. The cheapest soundbar starts at just $38. Here are the best soundbar sales to shop right now.
