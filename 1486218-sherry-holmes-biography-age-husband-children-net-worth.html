    Who is Sherry Holmes?

    Sherry Holmes is a television personality, construction contractor, DIY home improvement specialist, and philanthropist from Canada. Many also know her as one of the daughters of Canadian contractor/television host Mike Holmes Sr.

    How old is Sherry Holmes?

    Sherry Holmes’ age is 36 years as of 2023. She was born in Ontario, Canada, on 21 June 1987.

    Who is Sherry Holmes' father?

    Her father, Michael James Holmes, is a Canadian architect, builder/contractor, television host, businessman, and philanthropist. Mike Holmes Sr.'s first TV show, Holmes on Homes, premiered on HGTV Canada in 2001.     Her mum, Alexandra Lorex, is a Canadian-American. She married Mike Holmes Sr. in 1982 and divorced in 1993. Because of this, Mike's parents raised their three children.

    Who are Sherry Holmes' siblings?

    The Canadian TV personality is the second child in her family. Sherry Holmes' elder sister, Amanda Holmes, was born on 2 September 1984 in Canada, while her younger brother, Mike Holmes Jr., was born on 25 July 1989 in Canada.

    Amanda Holmes is known for featuring in Holmes on Homes (2001) and Holmes Family Rescue (2021) television shows on HGTV Canada. Meanwhile, Mike Holmes Jr. has appeared on Holmes on Homes (2001), Holmes Inspection (2009), and Holmes and Holmes (2016).

    Sherry Holmes' career history

    Sherry and her siblings grew up playing with their dad and watching him work. At 16, she began to work behind the scenes on construction projects that aired in his Holmes on Homes TV series.

    Her brother joined the crew in 2003 at age 14 before taking a three-year carpentry program at George Brown College. "Start ’em young" was the philosophy in the Holmes household.

    Mike Holmes Sr. started his construction career at age 19 and established his first contracting company two years later. He now owns three companies and around 13 TV shows, which have earned him multiple awards.

    The TV star officially launched Sherry's on-screen TV career in 2007. Since then, the lady has worked on multiple construction projects, including rebuilding homes for New Orleans residents in 2008 after the Hurricane Katrina floods hit the region.

    After the New Orleans project, Sherry became a permanent member of her dad's Holmes Makes It Right construction crew. Also, she has appeared alongside her father and brother in at least seven construction TV shows.

    Sherry also joined the cast of HGTV’s Home to Win in 2021. Social issues like sexism in the industry and the lingering stigma that skilled trades are a "fallback career that just men do" inspired her to expand her skill set at age 21.
