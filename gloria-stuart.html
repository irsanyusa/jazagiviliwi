    Profession: Actress

    Biography: Hollywood career spanned (with a long break in the middle) from 1932 until 2004 where she appeared on stage, television and in film, for which she was best-known. Appeared as Claude Rains' sweetheart in "The Invisible Man", and as the elderly Rose Dawson Calvert in the Academy Award-winning film "Titanic".

    She is the oldest person to be nominated in a supporting role for a competitive Oscar, for her role in "Titanic", at the age of 87.

    Born: July 4, 1910
    Birthplace: Santa Monica, California, USA

    Generation: Greatest Generation
    Chinese Zodiac: Dog
    Star Sign: Cancer

    Died: September 26, 2010 (aged 100)
