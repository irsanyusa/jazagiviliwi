    Gwen Stefani is 51 years old and looks as gorgeous as ever sporting some very iconic fashion statements. She is gearing up for season 19 of The Voice and we cannot wait to see what she wears this season and if she will flash her toned abs. Is anyone wondering what Gwen eats in a day to keep her amazing figure?

    Gwen Stefani Sticks To A Mostly Vegan Diet

    Gwen has been following a mostly vegan diet for the past few years. She has actually been a vegetarian since she was 12 years old. Though we don’t know exactly what Gwen eats for breakfast, it is assumed that she eats a lot of fresh fruits. Gwen spent the quarantine amid the coronavirus pandemic at boyfriend Blake Shelton’s Oklahoma ranch. The pair even planted watermelons together which leads us to assume they got to eat the fruits of their labor, literally.

    Gwen also loves coffee and tea so she most likely has one of the two for breakfast. In an interview with Bon Appétit, Gwen spoke about her love for teas. “This is going to sound crazy, but I have to have decaf English Breakfast tea with vanilla soy milk in my dressing room,” she said. “When it comes to touring or shows, it’s all about timing when you eat. If you eat too close to the show or you eat the wrong thing, it ruins the entire night. I have to be really strategic about how much I drink, how much I eat, what time I eat, and what I eat. If anything good goes down, it’s usually after.”

    Previously, TMZ obtained a list of Gwen’s tour demands and she asked for fruits, teas and an assortment of vegetables and nuts. For lunch, celebrity chef Giada De Laurentiis said Gwen also likes to eat healthy. Blake on the other hand loves fried food. “I grill a lot of steaks and things, but my favorite is chicken fried steak, mashed potatoes and country gravy,” he told Country Living. He also admitted that he loves snacking on Cheetos.

    Regardless of what’s being cooked, we would love a seat at Blake and Gwen’s dinner table. Check out the What’s Hot Video below to see how their five-year relationship evolved since they met on The Voice. The power couple recently bought a home together in Encino, California and have had engagement rumors swirling for years.

    Gwen Loves One Particular Dish That Her Family Makes

    When it comes time for the holidays, there’s one dish that Gwen’s family makes every year. “We actually eat lasagna,” she told Bon Appétit. “I don’t know when that happened. My mom makes lasagna and she’ll make one with meat and one with just ricotta cheese. I love that.” Obviously, with Gwen’s meat-free diet, she favors the one with the cheese.

    Gwen also loves pizza just like the rest of us. “After a Chicago show we literally came home with nine seven-pound Chicago deep dish pizzas and ate a few on the plane,” she said. “The next day I had my entire family over and we went to town on the rest of them.” That sounds like pure bliss.
