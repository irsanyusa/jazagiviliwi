    An unidentified man has gone viral for converting his pickup car into a temporary swimming pool.

    In a video seen on Twitter, the man laid a big strong nylon over the truck's bed and filled it with water to give it the semblance of a pool.

    At the centre of the car swimming pool, he placed a small table containing drinks and edibles.

    In the clip, he swam and took a bite out of the edible on the table while taking a break from swimming.
