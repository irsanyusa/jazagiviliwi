    26 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Full Name: Onika Tanya Maraj
    Profession: Rapper and Singer

    Biography: Minaj lived with grandmother in Trinidad until the age of 5, when she and her brother re-joined their parents in Queens, New York City. She graduated from Fiorello LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts, in Manhattan.

    Minaj has sold over 100 million records worldwide. Behind only Taylor Swift, Minaj is the female artist with the second-most entries on the Hot 100 (including solo and features), accumulating over 100 chart entries. Her nineteen top 10 singles are the most for any female rapper.

    She has won many awards, including: 8 American Music Awards; 12 BET Awards; 5 MTV Awards; 4 Billboard Music Awards; and a Brit Award. Billboard ranked her as No. 7 in their Top 10 Female Artists of the 2010's, as well as the No. 1 female rapper.

    Born: December 8, 1982
    Birthplace: Saint James, Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago
    Age: 41 years old
