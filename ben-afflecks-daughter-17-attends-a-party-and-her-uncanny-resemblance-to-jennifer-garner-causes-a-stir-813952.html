    06 Aug 2024, 00:00

    “I thought it was Jennifer!” this phrase probably crossed most minds when they looked at Violet Affleck. Social media users were amazed by his 17-year-old daughter’s striking resemblance to her mother, Jennifer Garner, so much that she was even called her twin.

    Violet is his daughter from the previous marriage.

    The couple met during the filming of Pearl Harbor and 4 years later their relationship began. They got married in June 2005 and welcomed 3 children — daughters Violet Anne and Seraphina Rose Elizabeth and son Samuel.

    She attended a recent party.

    Violet went to a billionaire CEO Michael Rubin’s Fourth of July party on the weekend. Jennifer Lopez, Ben Affleck and his daughter posed together for a few snaps. One photo in particular went viral where Ben was standing in the middle, Violet on the right and J. Lo on the left.

    The resemblance caused a stir on social media.

    Once the snaps appeared on social media, the users quickly noticed that Violet was a spitting image of Jennifer Garner. Someone wrote, “I thought this was Jen, I was so confused.” Another user joked, “I wish you lied and said it was her. That would have been hilarious.” Another added, “I thought this was an awkward picture between wife and ex-wife for a moment.”

    She’s on good terms with J. Lo.

    ROBERTO SCHMIDT/AFP/East News

    Violet has a good relationship with her stepmom Jennifer Lopez. The star made a big effort to get to know Ben’s kids, so they bonded quickly. J. Lo also wanted to be a source of strength for his and Jennifer Garner’s kids.
