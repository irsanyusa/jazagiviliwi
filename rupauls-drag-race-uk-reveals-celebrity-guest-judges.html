    The wait is nearly over for fans of the electrifying and fabulous reality show, RuPaul’s Drag Race UK. With anticipation at an all-time high, the hit series is set to make its triumphant return to BBC Three and BBC iPlayer this autumn, promising a fresh dose of charisma, uniqueness, nerve, and talent that viewers have come to adore. This season is bound to be a glittering spectacle, as a stunning array of celebrity guest judges and special guests take their seats at the judge’s table and beyond.

    RuPaul’s Drag Race UK Will Feature a Star-Studded Cast Of Guest Judges

    As the stage is ready for another exhilarating season, the show introduces a star-studded array of guest judges and special guests who will add their own flair and expertise to the competition. Joining the illustrious Drag Icon and head judge, RuPaul Charles, are nine exceptional guest judges, each a luminary in their respective fields.

    From comedian and actor Aisling Bea to pop sensation Alexandra Burke, the panel boasts an ensemble of voices that will enhance the judging experience. The wit and wisdom of actor Cush Jumbo, the artistic vision of designer Daphne Guinness, and also the comedic charm of actor and presenter Joel Dommett are just a few examples of the dynamic energy these judges bring.

    This talented group of individuals brings a diverse set of skills and experiences to the show. Supermodel Kristen McMenamy contributes her fashion expertise, pop icon Sophie-Ellis Bextor her musical legacy, BAFTA winner Suranne Jones her acting acumen, and actor Yasmin Finney her fresh perspective. Together, they promise to provide valuable feedback and enrich the show’s critiques for the competing queens.

    The queens will face a series of challenges designed to push their drag skills to the absolute limit. While the final verdict rests with RuPaul and her loyal Drag Race UK confidantes, Michelle Visage, Alan Carr, and Graham Norton, the journey to determine the fate of these queens wouldn’t be complete without the input of an ensemble of celebrity Drag Race super fans.

    The Show Will Also Have Celebrity Cameos

    In addition to the guest judges, several celebrities will make cameo appearances to boost the competition’s excitement. The Werk Room will welcome the renowned Edward Enninful OBE, who will provide the queens with fashion advice and a delightful furry companion. Moreover, the iconic Snatch Game challenge will feature the comedic talents of Carol Vorderman and Alexandra Burke.

    Additionally, the queens will receive choreography guidance from beloved Strictly Come Dancing star Karen Hauer. Joining Hauer will be choreographer extraordinaire Claudimar Neto, who will ensure their performances reach mesmerizing heights.

    RuPaul’s Drag Race UK, commissioned by Fiona Campbell and Kalpna Patel-Knight, celebrates diversity, creativity, and self-expression. Executive Producers at World of Wonder, including RuPaul , work hard behind the scenes to bring this spectacular show to life.
