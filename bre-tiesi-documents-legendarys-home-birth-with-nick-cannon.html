    The Masked Singer host Nick Cannon has officially welcomed his first child with model Bre Tiesi. The model revealed the arrival of their son via social media on Monday.

    Bre Tiesi Welcomes First Child With Nick Cannon in At Home Birth

    On July 25, Bre Tiesi shared multiple photos of her labor journey on Instagram, multiple include Nick Cannon. Within the post’s caption, she reveals that she gave birth to Legendary in an all natural unmedicated home birth.

    “This experience has changed me forever and I couldn’t of asked for a more amazing and supportive partner,” Tiesi wrote about Cannon. “Daddy showed the f up for us.. I couldn’t of done it without you.”

    Within the comments, Cannon is seen praising Tiesi for her delivery. He complimented her passion drive, focus, brilliance, and love.

    “Honored and privileged to experience this beautiful miracle with you!” Cannon wrote. “Thank you for the greatest gift any human can give to another. For this my love is forever indebted.”

    For those who are interested, Tiesi’s birth video is now live on YouTube. The video gained over 118,000 views after it’s initial release. This intimate video gives tons of information about their son, including his exact birth date and time. Legendary was born on June 28 at 1:28 am.

    The 11-minute birth video also reveals that the baby had respiratory issues during the delivery. Legendary was born with a “long crown.” This condition is typically caused by a nuchal hand or a hand by the ear. The model immediately knew something was off when Legendary wasn’t crying. Her midwife quickly went into action, giving the child an air tube. After a few moments, he began to cry. At the end of the video, the model acknowledges that Legendary is doing “perfect.”

    Nick Cannon Has Other Children on The Way

    Although Legendary is Cannon’s eighth child, it isn’t his last. The Masked Singer host provided confirmation in early June that he is expecting another child with DJ Abby De La Rosa. It’s possible he will welcome other children this year as well, since he’s previously teased the birth of three children.

    In late January, Cannon confirmed that he was expecting his first child with Teisi. This announcement came soon after the loss of Cannon’s son Zen with Alyssa Scott. At the time, he acknowledged that Tiesi was respectful to Scott following the death of their son. Zen passed away at just five months old after a battle with brain cancer. Cannon also shares 10 year old twins Monroe and Moroccan with ex-wife Mariah Carey; four-year-old Golden “Sagon” and 1-year-old Powerful Queen with Brittany Bell; and 7-month-old twins, Zion Mixolydian and Zillion Heir, with Abby De La Rosa.
