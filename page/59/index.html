Full Name: Phyllis Latour Doyle Profession: Secret Agent Biography: Pippa Latour was the last surviving member of the group of 39 female secret agents who served in WW2 as a Special Operations Agents in Sir Winston Churchill's 'secret army'. A member of the British Women's Auxiliary Air Force (WAAF), her fluency in French led to her being tapped for Special Operations Executive (SOE) training school. Instruction there included lessons in parachuting, weapons, explosives, unarmed combat, and wireless and telegraphy.
