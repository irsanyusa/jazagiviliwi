    For actors and pop stars, a bright appearance is an essential element that their career often depends on. So, it’s no wonder why celebrities do everything possible to look their best with the help of cosmetic procedures and even plastic surgery. But some famous women are against these improvements and choose to age naturally.

    Claudia Schiffer

    Mary Evans / AF Archive / Graham Whitby Boot. / East News, Jason Mendez / Everett Collection / East News

    The model admits that she’s never used any invasive cosmetic procedures and is more afraid of Botox than of her wrinkles. She said she would never have plastic surgery because she worries about the long-term effects. Also, she believes that aging is natural, and everyone must accept that looks will change.

    Julia Roberts

    John Barrett / PHOTOlink.net / East News, Sebastien Courdji / KCS / East News

    The famous actress is skeptical about Botox and plastic surgery. She says she feels sorry for women who don’t give themselves a chance to see what they’ll look like as older people. She wants her children to see the natural emotions on her face.

    Jennifer Lopez

    EVERETT COLLECTION / EAST NEWS, Invision / Invision / East News

    Jennifer Lopez denies having Botox injections or plastic surgery. Once, fans noticed that her forehead didn’t move, meaning that she’d had Botox. But the singer snapped, saying that it was just her face and that she had never done Botox, injectables, or surgery because she feels beautiful in her own skin.

    Amanda Peet

    Mary Evans / Allstar / Graham Whitby Boot. / East News, ANGELA WEISS / AFP / East News

    In an interview, Amanda said that she’d never done Botox or fillers or anything invasive and was not going to start doing them.

    Meryl Streep

    EMI / UNIVERSAL / Album / EAST NEWS, VALERIE MACON / AFP / East News

    The actress is decidedly against plastic surgery because it can go wrong. According to Meryl, changing your face with the help of plastic surgery is like wearing a veil.

    Gillian Anderson

    PHOTOlink / Everett Collection / East News, Stuart Hardy / ABACAPRESS.COM / Abaca / East News

    When fans began to discuss Gillian’s youthful complexion after the release of the X Files reboot, the actress said that she didn’t care what other people said about her. She knew she didn’t do any plastic surgery, and that was that.

    Rachel Hunter

    Mary Evans / Allstar / Graham Whitby Boot. / East News, LISA O'CONNOR / AFP / East News

    Previously, this model had nothing against plastic surgery and even thought of having it. But traveling the world has changed her views.

    Mandy Moore

    EAST NEWS, Invision / Invision / East News

    Once, the actress was accused of having a nose job. She was startled because she thought her nose was far from perfect. And if she had done something like that, she would have fixed it. She also said that people will believe what they want, and that’s fine.

    Emma Thompson

    BBC / Courtesy Everett Collection / East News, AP / Associated Press / East News

    Emma feels plastic surgery, like Botox, is “psychotic.” She also believes that making young people think that plastic surgery is normal is a bad idea.

    Ashley Judd

    Photo By Michael Ferguson / PHOTOlink.net / East News, Evan Agostini / Invision / AP / East News

    Ashley was also accused of having plastic surgery. In response, the actress published a long letter stating that this wasn’t true and that her face looked puffy because she was sick.

    Kate Beckinsale

    EVERETT COLLECTION / EAST NEWS, River / MEGA / Mega Agency / East News

    The actress revealed that she plans to age naturally and will not go under the knife. She doesn’t like surgically enhanced women in Hollywood and wants to look like her mother.

    Liv Tyler

    CJ Contino / Everett Collection / EAST NEWS, Invision / Invision / East News

    Liv said that she would never have plastic surgery. She doesn’t like how people change after having it.

    Frances Conroy

    Mary Evans / Allstar / Graham Whitby Boot. / East News, Invision / Invision / East News

    Frances is against plastic surgery. She feels that getting any surgery or Botox injections is similar to wearing a mask.

    Jennifer Aniston

    PHOTOlink / Everett Collection / East News, Invision / Invision / East News

    The actress had a nose job once to fix her deviated septum. And this is all she’s ever done to her face. She is not against plastic surgery and doesn’t judge those who’ve had it, but she prefers LED light therapy, facial massages, and good creams for herself.

    Brigitte Bardot at 25 and 72

    The legendary French actress is known for her natural beauty. Brigitte believes she can use her money for a better purpose than plastic surgery.

    What do you think about plastic surgery and beauty injections? Do you think we should age naturally, or that we can turn to specific procedures to stay younger looking longer?
