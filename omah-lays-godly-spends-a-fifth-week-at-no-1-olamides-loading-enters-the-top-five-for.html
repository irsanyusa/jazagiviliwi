    Omah Lays Godly Spends a Fifth Week at No. 1, Olamides Loading Enters the Top Five

    The Top 50 blends all-genre freemium streaming (Top Streaming Chart), radio airplay (Top Airplay Chart) and TV airplay (TV Top Songs Chart) in Nigeria. All charts will be dated and updated on Mondays, this weeks chart will be revealed on Monday, January 4.

    10 Jul 2024, 00:00

    The Top 50 blends all-genre freemium streaming (Top Streaming Chart), radio airplay (Top Airplay Chart) and TV airplay (TV Top Songs Chart) in Nigeria. All charts will be dated and updated on Mondays, this week’s chart will be revealed on Monday, January 4.

    'Godly,' released under KeyQaad/SIRE/Warner, tallied a fourth week at No. 1 on radio chart with 45.8 million radio airplay audience impression (down 2.96%). It stays atop the streaming chart for a third week with 2.75 million equivalent streams (down 1.78%) and jumps to No. 15 on TV chart with 5.19 million TV airplay audience impression (up 64.24%).

    As a result, 'Godly' becomes the last No. 1 song of 2020 and the first No. 1 of 2021.

    ADVERTISEMENT

    Olamide’s 'Infinity' featuring Omah Lay keeps at its No. 2 peak for a seventh week; it tallied 33.91 million radio airplay audience impression (up 11.98%) to move 4-3 on radio chart. The song drew 1.97 million equivalent streams (down 1.99 %) to stay at No. 2 on streaming chart and tallied 6.33 million TV airplay audience impression (down 7.32%) dropping 8-11 on TV chart.

    Bella Shmurda’s 'Cash App' with Zlatan & Lincoln holds at its peak of No. 3 after drawing 28.94 million radio airplay audience impression during the tracking week. The entire top 3 remains unchanged week-over-week.

    Davido’s 'The Best' featuring Mayorkun, which peaked at No. 1 on the chart dated November 30, 2020, rises 5-4 on the chart. Olamide’s 'Loading' featuring Bad Boy Timz moves to No. 5 maintaining its feat of never dropping to a lower position on the Top 50.

    'Loading' rises to No. 1 on TV Top Songs chart with 11.05 million TV airplay audience impression – becoming the first No. 1 for both artiste on TV chart.

    ADVERTISEMENT

    Rounding out this week’s top ten of the top 50; Davido’s 'Jowo' drops 4-6 after peaking at No. 3, Patoranking’s 'Abule' moves 8-7; Mayorkun’s 'Your Body' ascends 11-8 after debuting and peaking at No. 6. Two formers No. 1s in Davido’s 'FEM' and Wizkid’s 'Ginger' featuring Burna Boy occupy the last two positions of the top ten.

    The first issue of TurnTable Magazine was launched on Friday, December 18 which includes the End of the Year Charts 2020. You can check it on TurnTable.

    All chart news will be available @turntablecharts on both Twitter and Instagram while component charts such as Top Streaming Chart and Top Airplay Chart; and the second issue of the Annual Holidays Chart will be updated on Tuesday, January 5, 2020.
